# Sentiment Analysis of DXY and Bitcoin

## 📌 Objective
To extract and quantify sentiment from financial news and social media for DXY and Bitcoin, exploring its potential as a market signal.

## 📊 Data
- Sources: (e.g., financial news APIs, Twitter, Reddit)
- Assets: DXY and Bitcoin
- Frequency: Daily headlines or posts

## ⚙️ Methodology
1. Text preprocessing: tokenization, stopword removal, lemmatization
2. Sentiment scoring: VADER / TextBlob / custom sentiment dictionary
3. Aggregation: daily sentiment scores per asset
4. Visualization: sentiment trends vs. price movements
5. Optional analysis: correlation or lagged impact of sentiment on price

## 📈 Results
- Plotted sentiment time series alongside asset prices
- Observed periods where sentiment spikes coincide with price moves
- Insights into lagged effects of sentiment on DXY and BTC

## 💡 Insights
- Sentiment can provide early-warning signals of market momentum
- DXY and BTC react differently to positive/negative sentiment
- Combining alternative data sources strengthens predictive power

## 🧰 Tools Used
- Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, nltk, finBert)
- Jupyter Notebook
